# InvenTrack 📦

> *"Every business — from a solo founder to a 500-person operation — deserves real-time inventory intelligence."*

A full-featured, cloud-native **Inventory Management Platform** designed to compete directly with market leaders like Zoho Inventory, Cin7, Fishbowl, and NetSuite — built for small-to-mid-sized businesses (SMBs) and growing enterprises.

---

## 🎯 Why InvenTrack?

Market research shows **43% of companies still rely on spreadsheets** for inventory management. InvenTrack solves this by consolidating the most critical capabilities into a single, intuitive, and affordable platform.

**Target Verticals:** Retail (omnichannel) · Wholesale & Distribution · Light Manufacturing · eCommerce

---

## ✨ Core Features (MVP)

### 📋 Item & SKU Management
- Product variants (color, size, material) with per-variant SKUs and pricing
- Composite items / bundles with auto stock deduction
- Bulk CSV/Excel import-export with field mapping
- Full item activity audit log

### 🏭 Multi-Warehouse Inventory Tracking
- Real-time stock updates across multiple locations
- Reorder point rules with **automatic Purchase Order generation**
- Batch and serial number tracking
- Low-stock alerts and thresholds per item per warehouse

### 📦 Order Management
- **Sales Order lifecycle:** Create → Approve → Fulfill → Invoice → Payment
- **Purchase Order lifecycle:** Create → Send to Vendor → Receive → Bill
- Barcode / QR code scanning (web camera, mobile, USB scanner)

### 📊 Dashboard & Reports
- 10 customizable KPI widgets
- 20+ built-in reports with CSV / Excel / PDF export
- Real-time COGS, valuation & audit trail

### 🔗 Integrations
- **eCommerce:** Shopify, WooCommerce
- **Accounting:** QuickBooks Online, Xero
- **Shipping:** FedEx, UPS, USPS, DHL (multi-carrier label printing)
- **API:** RESTful API v1 with OAuth 2.0 + Webhooks

### 📱 Mobile
- iOS and Android apps with barcode scanning support
